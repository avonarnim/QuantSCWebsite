Education

We teach members all they need to know about finance. We look for students with a general interest in the field of finance and quantitative skills in the areas of mathematics, statistics, and computer science. Successful members of our organization enter with little investment knowledge but come ready to learn.
Our goal is to educate members about the principles of strategic investing through a weekly lecture series, guest speaker events, and engaging research.

FAQ

Does Quant SC accept members on a rolling basis?

Due to the structure of our organization's projects, we are unable to accept members outside of the Fall application cycle.

Do I need any prior finance/tech experience?

Nope, as long as you demonstrate an eagerness to learn, we will teach you everything you need to know.
